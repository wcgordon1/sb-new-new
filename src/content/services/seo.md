## Making your site discoverable

We align technical SEO, content, and analytics so your site can be found by the right people. Every change is measured, so you understand what is moving the needle and where to invest next.

### Our Approach

- **Audit and Baseline**: Technical crawl, performance review, and analytics health check.
- **Strategy and Roadmap**: Keyword research, content gaps, and IA updates prioritized by impact.
- **Execution and QA**: Implementing on-page improvements, schema, redirects, and UX fixes.
- **Reporting and Iteration**: Clear dashboards, experiment results, and next steps each cycle.

### Our Services

- **Technical SEO Foundation**: Sitemaps, robots, schema, redirects, and Core Web Vitals improvements.
- **On-Page Optimization**: Metadata, headings, internal linking, and layout adjustments for intent.
- **Content Planning**: Search-led topics, outlines, and briefs aligned to your product narrative.
- **Analytics Setup**: Events, goals, and dashboards to monitor growth and conversions.

### Why Choose Us?

- **Full-Stack Perspective**: Developers and strategists working together so fixes actually ship.
- **Measurable Delivery**: Every recommendation ties to KPIs with reporting you can share.
- **Sustainable Growth**: Systems and documentation that keep performance improving after engagement.
