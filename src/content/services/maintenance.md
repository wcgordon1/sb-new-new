## Reliable support after launch

We stay with you after launch to keep the experience smooth. Our maintenance plans cover monitoring, updates, and improvements so you focus on shipping new ideas without worrying about stability.

### Our Approach

- **Stabilize and Document**: Establish baselines, run health checks, and document dependencies.
- **Automate and Monitor**: Alerts for uptime, errors, and performance with clear escalation paths.
- **Plan and Ship**: Scheduled maintenance windows, content updates, and regression testing before release.
- **Review and Improve**: Regular reviews to tune performance, security, and accessibility.

### Our Services

- **Reliability Monitoring**: Uptime, logging, and error tracking with concise status updates.
- **Secure Updates**: Library upgrades, patching, backups, and credential management.
- **Content Support**: Publishing workflows, QA, and deployment of new pages or assets.
- **Performance Care**: Ongoing optimization for speed, SEO signals, and accessibility.

### Why Choose Us?

- **Hands-On Support**: The same team that builds keeps maintaining, so fixes are quick.
- **Predictable Process**: Clear schedules, communication, and rollback plans for every release.
- **Long-Term Stability**: Continuous improvements that protect performance as your product grows.
